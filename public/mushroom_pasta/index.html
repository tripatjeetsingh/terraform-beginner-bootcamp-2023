Mario's Mushroom Pasta

Enjoy Mario's Delicious Mushroom Pasta

- This pasta dish comes together faster than you can beat every level in Bowser’s Castle. Seriously, from start to finish, you’re looking at about a 30-minute process to make this pasta.

- Deglazing with white wine is a crucial step that adds depth and complexity to the mushroom pasta sauce. It loosens the flavorful caramelized bits from the bottom of the skillet, infusing the dish with a rich and nuanced taste.

- Once the wine has reduced, it’s time for the sauce to come together. That’s where the reserved pasta water comes in. When you boil the pasta, some of the starches leach into the water. Adding a small amount of this starchy water to the skillet helps the sauce adhere better to the pasta and provides thickness as it cools.

- Use a heatproof measuring cup to scoop out just enough pasta water to achieve the desired consistency. Simmer it with some heavy cream in the skillet for a silky-smooth mushroom cream sauce.
